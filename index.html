Where did the Nissan GT-R Originate From?

It start with an Automotive company named "Prince Motor Company" in Suginami, Tokyo, Japan that manufactured the first ever Nissan Skyline/GT-R named 2000gt. The assembly lines for the Gtr's started in 1966 but cars where not sold until 1969 because the Prine Motor Company declare bankruptcy and sold their company to the well known automotive industy, we all know as "Nissan Automotive Company".

Why did the Skyline GT-R become so Popular?

The Skyline GT-r became one the most iconic sports cars in the 90's and early 2000's because it was competing supercars such as Ferrari Testa d'Oro, F50, Lamborghini Diablo, and many more. The iconic part wasn't so much that the skyline kept up with these supercars, the thing that made the skyline's extraordinary was their price. The Premium 1996 Skyline r34/gtr cost a top price of 45,000 US dollars which was not cheap but compared to the supercars during that era it was the best bet.

Nissan Skyline vs Nissan Stagea

The first year the Nissan Stagea was introduce to the public was in 1996, the nissan stagea was manufactured for the common people that want a sports car/wagon but also a family car that could be used to transport and hual. The Nissan Stagea's were only manufactured from 1996 to 2006.
